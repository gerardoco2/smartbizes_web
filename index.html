information technology, consulting and outsourcing in various segments of industry like advertising, education, finance, healthcare, hotel, media and entertainment, mobile and wireless, retail, real estate, sports and fitness, technology, non-profit and more.
Our team of talented, dynamic and young professionals is capable of providing high and quality solutions to our clients with the right blend of technology, domain knowledge and effective methodology.
